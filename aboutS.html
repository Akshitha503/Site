About us

Beginning as a modest restaurant in 2016, Spice has grown into one of the most-admired restaurants in Hyderabad. Spice Den offers a unique fusion of several cuisines. At Spice Den experience a new level of dining with our delicious and appetizing vegetarian and non vegetarian dishes and enjoy the taste of global dishes. Our delicious and appetizing lunch and dinner menu is sure to appease your appetite with mouth watering courses. The lively ambience and the warm staff will leave a lasting impression on you and all. Our award-winning cuisine is loved for its purity and taste, true in every mouthful to its royal origins. Over the last four years, Spice Den won the hearts of guests in Hyderabad.
For the convenience and safety of our guests, we have valet parking and adequate security arrangements. We also practice the highest quality and hygiene standards to ensure our cuisine remains top-notch in all aspects. Industry best practices, world-class processes and cutting-edge technology enable our Outlets to set new benchmarks in the Food & Hospitality industry for their efficiency. We, however, believe that our greatest reward is the trust and loyalty bestowed on us by our delighted guests. We are led by a strong management team which is amongst the finest in the industry.
